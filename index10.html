Russian Military Assets

Exploring Russia's fighter jets, weapons, and military technology

Fighter Jets

Sukhoi Su-57

The Su-57 is Russia's fifth-generation stealth fighter jet, designed for air superiority and ground attack. It features advanced avionics, supercruise capability, and low observability.

Sukhoi Su-35

The Su-35 is a highly advanced multirole fighter jet that can perform air-to-air and air-to-ground combat missions. It has high agility, advanced radar systems, and enhanced weapons systems.

MiG-29

The MiG-29 is a versatile multi-role fighter known for its ability to operate from short airstrips and its excellent performance in dogfights. It has been widely used by Russian and foreign air forces.

Missiles and Weaponry

Iskander Missile System

The Iskander missile system is one of Russia's most advanced ballistic missile systems, capable of striking high-value targets with precision at long ranges. It is designed for quick deployment and versatility.

Tor-M2 Air Defense System

The Tor-M2 is a short-range air defense missile system designed to protect military units from air threats. It can engage aircraft, helicopters, and drones with high accuracy in all weather conditions.

S-400 Triumph

The S-400 is an advanced long-range surface-to-air missile system, capable of intercepting a wide range of airborne threats, including aircraft, missiles, and even stealth targets at extreme ranges.

Russian Naval Assets

Peter the Great (Kirov-class Cruiser)

The Peter the Great is a heavy nuclear-powered missile cruiser, one of the largest and most powerful warships in the Russian Navy. It is equipped with advanced radar systems, anti-ship, and anti-aircraft missiles.

Admiral Kuznetsov (Aircraft Carrier)

Russia's only aircraft carrier, the Admiral Kuznetsov is designed for long-range strikes and air superiority missions. It supports a wide range of fixed-wing and rotary-wing aircraft for various military operations.

Ground Forces

T-90 Main Battle Tank

The T-90 is Russia's most widely used main battle tank, featuring composite armor, a powerful 125mm smoothbore gun, and a high degree of mobility. It is a key asset in modern armored warfare.

Armata T-14

The Armata T-14 is a next-generation Russian main battle tank, featuring an unmanned turret and advanced protection systems, offering superior combat performance on the battlefield.
